About us

"San Isidro National High School, located in the of San Isidro Catanauan in Quezon, is a renowned educational institution committed to providing quality education and nurturing the young minds of its students. As a national high school, San Isidro offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines academic excellence with holistic development. The school takes pride in its dedicated faculty members who inspire and guide students towards achieving their full potential in various academic disciplines. Beyond academics, SINHS fosters a supportive and inclusive learning environment that promotes values such as teamwork, leadership, and social responsibility. Students are encouraged to engage in co-curricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural events, to explore their talents and interests. The school campus boasts modern facilities, well-equipped classrooms, and state-of-the-art laboratories to facilitate effective learning experiences. With a commitment to innovation and adaptability, San Isidro National High School embraces technological advancements, providing students with access to digital resources and promoting digital literacy. SINHS takes pride in its strong community partnerships and actively involves parents, guardians, and local organizations in the educational journey of its students. Through collaborative efforts, the school strives to create a supportive network that empowers students to become responsible citizens and future leaders. With its unwavering dedication to academic excellence, character development, and community engagement, SINHS continues to shape the lives of its students and contribute to the overall growth and progress of San Isidro Catanauan, Quezon.
